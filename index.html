Research Interests

My research focuses on developing robust and interpretable deep learning systems for classification, prediction, and change detection in spatiotemporal and network data. I am particularly interested in:

  • Seismic event classification (Earthquakes vs. Explosions)
  • Domain adaptation and generalization performance
  • Graph neural networks and network aggregation
  • Statistical modeling for change point detection in networks

Current Projects

  • Seismic Event Discrimination: Developing deep learning models trained on synthetic waveforms and tested on real seismic data
  • Change Point Detection in Networks: Using spectral embeddings and machine learning to detect temporal structural changes
  • Fair Aggregation Models: Investigating fairness-aware strategies for combining multiple social networks
